ABOUT

The Strokes are a rock band from New York that got really popular in the early 2000s. They started in 1998 and hit it big with their first album, *Is This It*, in 2001. The music had a simple, rough sound that people loved, with catchy guitar parts and laid-back singing from their lead singer, Julian Casablancas. Songs like "Last Nite" and "Someday" became super popular. Even though they've tried new things with their music over the years, they still keep that cool, classic vibe that made them famous, and they’re still a big name in indie rock..

Over the years, The Strokes have gone through ups and downs, but they've stayed a favorite for a lot of fans. Their sound mixes rock with a cool, almost effortless attitude that made them stand out from other bands. Even when they took breaks or tried new styles in their later albums, people still loved them because they stuck to what made them great—simple, catchy songs with a lot of energy. They're one of those bands that helped shape modern indie music, and their influence is still strong today.
